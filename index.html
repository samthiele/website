Hi, I'm Sam.

As a geologist, I'm fascinated by our wonderfully complex yet amazingly organised planet. I particularly enjoy the challenges earth scientists face when integrating and synthesising diverse and multi-scale datasets.

This has lead me to the cutting edge of digital geoscience, where I use data science and computer vision to capture and understand earth systems and processes.
